What this app does

Daily Planner helps you create daily plans, organize routines and tasks, check off what you finish, and see your progress clearly. It keeps everything simple, friendly, and useful for everyday life.

Create a plan for your day and keep your tasks organized.

📅

Organize your routine, appointments, errands, and reminders.

📈
Progress Tracking

View a clean progress bar for Today, Weekly, or Monthly progress.

Complete all tasks in a day to earn coins and unlock levels.

✎️
Edit Anytime
